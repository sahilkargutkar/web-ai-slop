Slop vs. Intentional Design

The skill doesn’t just flag problems — it helps rebuild with specific, contextual decisions.

AI Slop (Default Output)
  • Purple aurora gradient blob behind hero
  • Bento grid of rounded-square icon cards
  • "Unlock your [noun]" headline with rule-of-3 adjectives
  • Circular-avatar testimonials, no specific outcomes
  • 3-tier pricing with "Most Popular" highlighted
  • transition: all 0.3s ease on everything
  • Inter 400/600/700, default letter-spacing
Intentional Design
  • No decorative gradient — content leads
  • List layout or single focused CTA
  • Specific headline: "Saves 14h/week on invoice reconciliation"
  • Customer quotes with measurable before/after
  • Single plan or usage-based, no fake tiers
  • transition: opacity 0.2s on hover states only
  • Chosen typeface with tightened tracking on headings
